Introduction

Nice to “meet” you!!! My name is Mari Haruno Baker, but everyone calls me Haru or Haru-sensei. I've been teaching and supporting children with special needs and children who are difficult to go to school, or who don’t have confidence for over 15 years. I have been teaching Japanese to adults and kids for over 10 years. Also, I have supported Japanese people who are thinking to study abroad in Canada or who are already in canada for several years. I love teaching and motivating people!! Learning Japanese is great, but getting confidence to enjoy yourself in this society is the most important and in order to achieve the aims, I would like to support you and I always believe that is my mission!!
